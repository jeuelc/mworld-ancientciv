Early China

You probably know that China is one of the largest countries in the world. But did you know that Chinese civilisation has lasted from way back in prehistoric times to today?

As one of the oldest (if not the oldest) civilisations in the modern world, the roots of Chinese culture go back more than 5000 years!

The Chinese Empire was founded almost 2300 years ago. There were many periods of conflict and war in early China, including times when the unity of the empire was broken up. There were also many major cultural, artistic and technological innovations whose legacy continues today.
